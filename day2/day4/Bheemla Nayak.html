BHEEMLA NAYAK

DESCRIPTION

BHEEMLA NAYAK tells the story of an egoistic feud between two men. Daniel Shekar (Rana Daggubati) is the son of a politician and an entitled ex-army man, while Bheemla Nayak (Pawan Kalyan) is a principled police sub-inspector who detains Shekar on account of him smuggling liquor across the state border.
